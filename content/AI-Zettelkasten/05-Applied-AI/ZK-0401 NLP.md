**Definition**
- NLP enables machines to parse, generate, and reason over human language.

**Key Ideas**
- Covers tokenization, parsing, information extraction, and generation.
- Low-resource languages need transfer learning and targeted data collection.
- Evaluation must include cultural/linguistic error analysis.



**Real-World Use Cases**
- Parse citizen complaints submitted via SMS and WhatsApp.
- Extract clauses from procurement contracts for compliance checks.

**Technologies & Tooling**
- FastText and IndicBERT embeddings for low-resource languages.
- SpaCy pipelines with custom Sinhala/Tamil tokenizers.
