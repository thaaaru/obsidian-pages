**Definition**
- The AI risk landscape catalogs technical, ethical, and socio-economic hazards introduced by AI systems.

**Key Ideas**
- Core risk classes: safety, security, fairness, societal impact.
- Risk assessments align model capability with deployment context.
- Mitigation requires multi-layer controls spanning data, model, and process.

![AI Risk Classes](../99-Attachments/Diagrams/ai-risk-controls.svg)



**Real-World Use Cases**
- Publish national AI risk registers for cabinet oversight.
- Assess third-party vendor proposals for safety posture.

**Technologies & Tooling**
- Risk-tracking wikis (Confluence) tied to model inventories.
- Threat modeling templates in Open Policy Agent or STRIDE tools.
