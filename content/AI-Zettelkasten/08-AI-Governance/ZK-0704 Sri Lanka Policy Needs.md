**Definition**
- Identifies policy gaps and opportunities for Sri Lanka to steward AI adoption responsibly.

**Key Ideas**
- Need for national data governance, compute access, and talent pipelines.
- Regulatory sandboxes can accelerate pilots while managing risk.
- Public-sector procurement should reward open standards and transparency.



**Real-World Use Cases**
- Draft national AI action plans that balance innovation and safeguards.
- Coordinate academia-industry task forces around compute + talent gaps.

**Technologies & Tooling**
- Policy collaboration suites (Microsoft Loop, Coda) for inter-ministry drafting.
- Data catalogs (CKAN) to inventory public datasets for AI readiness.
