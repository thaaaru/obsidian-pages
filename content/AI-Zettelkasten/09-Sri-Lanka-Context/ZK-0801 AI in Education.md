**Definition**
- Explores AI use in Sri Lankan education for personalized learning, teacher support, and administration.

**Key Ideas**
- Language support must cover Sinhala, Tamil, and English content.
- Ethical guardrails needed for student data privacy and bias control.
- Teacher-facing copilots can reduce administrative workload.



**Real-World Use Cases**
- Adaptive learning portals that adjust to provincial curricula.
- Teacher copilots that auto-generate lesson plans and quizzes.

**Technologies & Tooling**
- LMS integrations via Moodle plugins calling LLM APIs.
- Speech-enabled classroom devices using Raspberry Pi + Whisper.
