**Definition**
- Quick-reference glossary for recurring AI terms across the vault.

**Key Ideas**
- AGI: Artificial General Intelligence capable of broad, human-level competence ([[ZK-0900 AGI]]).
- ASR: Automatic Speech Recognition systems turning audio into text ([[ZK-0402 Speech-Audio AI]]).
- Foundation Model: Large pre-trained model adaptable to many tasks ([[ZK-0300 Foundation Models]]).
- RAG: Retrieval-Augmented Generation coupling search with generation ([[ZK-0503 RAG]]).
- RLHF: Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback alignment method ([[ZK-0302 RLHF]]).
- Vector DB: Database optimized for similarity search over embeddings ([[ZK-0502 Vector Databases]]).



**Real-World Use Cases**
- Keep cabinet briefings aligned on terms like RAG or RLHF.
- Support translators when turning AI policy briefs into Sinhala and Tamil.
