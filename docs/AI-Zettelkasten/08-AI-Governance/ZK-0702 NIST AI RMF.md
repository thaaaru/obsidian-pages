**Definition**
- NIST AI RMF provides a voluntary framework to map, measure, manage, and govern AI risks.

**Key Ideas**
- Organized into Govern, Map, Measure, Manage functions.
- Emphasizes socio-technical considerations and documentation.
- Pairs with playbooks for different maturity levels.



**Real-World Use Cases**
- Map government agency responsibilities to RMF Govern/Map steps.
- Train audit teams using RMF-aligned tabletop exercises.

**Technologies & Tooling**
- RMF playbook templates in Notion or Confluence.
- Assessment platforms (Credo AI, Arthur) capturing controls evidence.
