**Definition**
- AI in Sri Lankan healthcare targets diagnostics, triage, and hospital resource planning.

**Key Ideas**
- Data harmonization across public/private hospitals is prerequisite.
- Telemedicine assistants need speech + text capabilities for rural clinics.
- Regulators require clinical validation and post-market surveillance.



**Real-World Use Cases**
- Radiology triage for tuberculosis or dengue complications.
- Hospital bed allocation forecasting in public hospitals.

**Technologies & Tooling**
- DICOM-compatible AI viewers (Arterys, MD.ai).
- FHIR APIs connecting AI services to HIS/EHR stacks.
